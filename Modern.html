I was taking some shots for my friends work and she had this ring that she was working on with for her business and as I saw it ideas came to my mind and I started to explore the possibility of what I can do with this silver chameleon ring which is fascinating just to look at so I decided to put it on a cactus and give it a touch of its natural habitat.

This is a portrait of the God 'Swet Bhairav' located in Basanatpur, Kathmandu, Nepal. It is a historic and religious figure situated in the heart of the city a very beautifully carved statue of the diety who is known as a diety of the Rain God 'Indra'. It is a beautiful work of art which no one can resist to admire and i fell one of its victim and had to capture its essence through my lens.
